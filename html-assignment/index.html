ABOUT ME

hello, I'm gazel, I was born and raised in a beautiful place called Kerala, India. I was always been interested in taking videos while I was a kid and that interest turned into a passion for videography. I'm a big soccer fan and I love to play whenever I get the chance and I try to enjoy every day of my life with the motto YOLO in my mind.

I've always dreamed of showing my stories through videography with a unique style to it, so I'm constantly working toward that dream a chasing it. when I'm not behind the lens, you'll find me on the soccer field, enjoying every moment of it. I achieved a few titles with my high school soccer team when I was in 11th grade. but the most memorable one was winning the Reliance Foundation District trophy.
